Memorize This!

Once you finish memorizing your times table, you are ready to moveon to larger and more complicated problems. To start, you should practice multiplying two digit numbers. There are many methods to do this, but they all rely on the partial products method. This involves multiplying the first digit of one number by the other number, multiplying the second digit of the first number by the other number, and adding the results together. Here is how you could multiply 72 by 48:

  1. Do 72*40=2880
  2. Do 72*8=576
  3. Add 2880 and 576
  4. The answer is 3456

However, that involves doing some tough problems. Here is an easier way:

  1. Do 72*50 as 72*100/2=3600
  2. Do 72*2=144
  3. Subtract 144 from 3600
  4. The answer is 3600.
